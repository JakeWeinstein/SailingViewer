When filtered users has items, render an absolute-positioned dropdown (bottom of textarea, z-50, bg-white shadow-lg border rounded-lg max-h-48 overflow-y-auto) listing each user with displayName and @username in muted text. Highlight selectedIndex item with bg-blue-50. Click on item calls selectUser.
   - The textarea gets all standard attributes (value, onChange, placeholder, rows, className).
